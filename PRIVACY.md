# Privacy Policy for InterceptX

Last updated: 2026-07-10

InterceptX does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing information. We value your privacy, and our extension is designed to operate completely locally on your device.

## What Data We Process and Store

### Saved Profiles and Rules
- **Data Description**: The names of your profiles, custom HTTP header names and values (both request and response directions), operation types (Set, Append, Remove), URL filter patterns, and enabled resource types.
- **Storage Location**: This data is stored locally in your browser's extension storage area using `chrome.storage.local`.
- **Purpose**: This configuration data is required to generate the network modification rules that customize header injection according to your specifications.
- **Transmission**: This configuration data is **never** transmitted to any remote servers, external APIs, or third parties. It remains strictly on your local browser profile.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use cookies, analytics scripts, trackers, or any other third-party services.

## Data Sharing
No data is shared with third parties under any circumstances. We do not sell, rent, trade, or distribute your configurations or usage metadata.

## Data Retention and Deletion
Your configuration profiles are retained in your browser's local storage for as long as the extension is installed. You can delete individual profiles or clear all data at any time through the extension's dashboard interface, or by uninstalling the extension.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with a new "Last updated" date.
